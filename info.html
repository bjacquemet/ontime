Concept

The idea of this extension is to display the next arrivals of your prefered map directly on a clock.

The minute of the 3 next arrivals, in a range of 30 minutes, are displayed. To help visualize the first arrival, the 2nd and 3rd are more displayed with more transparency.

About

This Chrome Extension is based on Nextbus Data, center on the city of San Francisco.
It's a simple experiment; any suggestion regarding the service / usage / concept is welcome!

The source code is available here. Feel free to modify it!
